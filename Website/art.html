How to commission

You can contact me through email.

In your message please provide the following information:

  • Type of your commission (avatar, basic art piece, animation)
  • Description of your commission
  • Your name and/or nickname on social media
  • Do you wish to be tagged/mentioned on my Instagram/Twitter once I post the picture online
  • Any additional information you wish me to know

I accept PayPal only and I take the payment in advance.
